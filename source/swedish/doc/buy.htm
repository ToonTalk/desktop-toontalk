Is ToonTalk for sale?

Currently ToonTalk is only for sale in Sweden. We are at various stages of negotiation with publishers in Japan, North America, and South America. If you are a software publisher and are interested in ToonTalk please contact us.

Logotron, a division of Addison Wesley Longman, in the UK has announced that they will start selling ToonTalk in during the summer of 1998. They have the rights to sell ToonTalk in Europe (except for Sweden), Africa, Australia, New Zealand, Hong Kong, Singapore, India, and Malaysia.

We are planning a product release this summer for all countries not covered by publishing contracts. Details will be announced soon.
